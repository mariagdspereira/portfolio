Villa Carvoeiro

Brand Identity of a (fictional) Airbnb in the South of Portugal, Algarve. "Villa Carvoeiro" The inspiration for this project was my grandfather's house typical chimney of Algarve, this house was were I spent my happiest days in and my favourite place in the world! I started doing this project just for fun but i thought it really turned out something interesting, my mother and I always dreamed of running a "bed and breakfast" so I thought, why not combine both?
